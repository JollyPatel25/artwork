	Painting allows children an educational opportunity that is also fun and exciting.
	Painting aids children acquire hand-eye coordination, an important skill in their age.
	This is developed while they learn to paint the parts that they see making sure their 
	hand movement is at par with their vision.Painting aids your child develop mobility 
	skills. Their hand muscles are being used, which allows them a scope to develop both 
	mentally and physically.
